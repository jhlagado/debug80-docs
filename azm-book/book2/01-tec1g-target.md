# Chapter 1 — The TEC-1G Target

This chapter will introduce the TEC-1G as the concrete machine for Book 2.

Planned coverage:

- MON-3 as the monitor environment
- the `$4000` user-code workbench region
- RAM, ROM, shadow, protect, and expansion regions
- keypad input and MON-3 key codes
- the six-digit seven-segment display
- the HD44780 LCD
- the 8x8 RGB matrix
- the one-bit speaker
- the ports that matter first

The chapter will keep the hardware model practical: what address or port you touch, what changes, and how Debug80 lets you see it.
