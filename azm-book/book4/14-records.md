# Chapter 14 — Records with `.type`

This chapter will document AZM record layouts: `.type`, fields, nested fields, `sizeof`, `offset`, and using record offsets with ordinary Z80 indexed addressing.

## Planned sections

- `.type` and `.endtype`
- `.byte`, `.word`, `.addr`, and `.field`
- Nested records
- `sizeof(Type)`
- `offset(Type, field)`
- Allocating one record
- Allocating arrays of records
- IX/IY field access with named offsets
